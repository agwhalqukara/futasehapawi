Ardyth Prather Jr and Ardyth Prather III shot dead by Austin Prather In Science Hill area

On November 7th, 2023, a shooting occurred in the Science Hill area of Pulaski County, Kentucky resulting in the deaths of Ardyth Prather Jr. and Ardyth Prather III. The suspect, identified as Austin Prather, is still at large and wanted by police. Read More.

Ardyth Prather Jr and Ardyth Prather III shot dead:

According to Sheriff Bobby Jones, deputies responded to the scene after reports of gunfire. Upon arrival, they found Ardyth Prather Jr. and Ardyth Prather III deceased. A third individual was also injured in the shooting.

Authorities believe Austin Prather, the 20-year-old grandson and son of the deceased, is responsible for the shootings. An arrest warrant has been issued charging him with murder.

The Search for Austin Prather

Prather fled the area after the shootings in a brown 2013 Jeep Wrangler. He is considered armed and dangerous.

Multiple law enforcement agencies, including the Pulaski County Sheriff’s Office and Somerset Police Department, are involved in the search. As of now, his whereabouts remain unknown.

Police are urging the public to contact them if they have any information on Prather’s location. Tips can be called in anonymously.

The tragic shootings in Science Hill have left two dead and one injured. Police continue to search for suspect Austin Prather, who they believe killed his father and grandfather.

Anyone who sees him or knows where he may be should contact authorities immediately and with extreme caution, as he is considered armed and dangerous. This remains an ongoing investigation.
