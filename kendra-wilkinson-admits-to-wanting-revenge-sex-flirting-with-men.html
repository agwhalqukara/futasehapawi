Kendra Wilkinson Admits to Wanting Revenge Sex, Flirting With Men

Kendra Wilkinson decided to forgive her husband, Hank Baskett, following his June 2014 cheating scandal, but that doesn’t mean she didn’t seek out other men. In an exclusive sneak peek of Friday’s episode of Kendra on Top, the former Playboy Playmate admits that she contemplates having revenge sex and that texting with former flings helps boost her confidence.

“When I went down that aisle and I married [Hank], I said to myself, ‘I’m leaving everything behind I’m never looking at another guy in my life, this is the man,’ and now because of the shake-up, I looked at other men again,” the mom to Hank Jr., 5, and Alijah, 15 months, tells her friends in the clip. “It just sucks being hurt.”

Wilkinson’s confidence boost came when she “woke up from the misery one day,” “looked in the mirror,” and felt “I still got it.”

“I’m about to go get it,” she recalled feeling. “I’m like, ‘Oh my god, this makes me feel confident and young.’ I want to feel sexy again.”
