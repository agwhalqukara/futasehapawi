Magician Groom Levitates During His Wedding First Dance: See the Pics

Weddings can be magical, but for one couple they literally were. Magician, Justin Willman, made sure his Labor Day wedding to photographer Jillian Sipkins was one of the most memorable nuptials ever — and they have the photos to prove it.

The couple, who shared a Drunk History-esque imitation of how they first met on YouTube, posted some incredible wedding photos captured to illustrate just how amazing their reception was.

Naturally, the charismatic pair chose to set their first dance to “I Put a Spell on You,” which went perfectly with Willman’s levitating dance move. Please notice the groom’s father’s reaction (top right) while his son performs the magical act in the photo, taken by Enrico de la Vega. “My dad’s reaction. Worth every penny,” Willman captioned the priceless pic. “She put a spell on me,” he shared with another angle of the routine.

More images of the couples’ first dance filled up the newlyweds’ clever wedding hashtag, #JustinTakesASip, featuring Willman casting a spell over Sipkins’ head with magic hands.

Of course the cake-cutting couldn’t be the average affair after a performance like that, so Willman and Sipkins, chopped their dessert with a long saber sword. “I HAVE A WIFE NOW,” Willman wrote along with the snap.
