Union J are a four-piece boyband, known for their hits including ‘You Got It All’ and ‘Carry You’. They first rose to fame on series nine of The X Factor UK in 2012, after Josh, Jaymi and JJ (who auditioned as Triple J), joined forces with solo contestant George Shelley. They placed fourth on the show and went on to release five singles and two albums, and also headlined two tours. In 2016, George Shelley quit the band to pursue his own endeavours, and was replaced by Casey Johnson, former member of 2014 X Factor boyband Stereo Kicks.

Now, Union J are back and stronger than ever, with Josh, Jaymi, JJ and Casey having recently completed a successful UK tour. New music is due soon, which makes the band’s arrival onto Snapchat even more exciting! We can’t wait to see what antics the boys get up to and we also hope for some more new music sneak peeks.
