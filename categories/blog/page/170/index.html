Qwabe Twins - Life and Family of Viggy and Virginia the Hamba Singers

published on July 13, 2024
The Qwabe Twins (born in 1997) are a group of sisters named Viggy and Virginia who were born in 1997 and rose to fame as contestants on season 15 of the Idols South Africa. The duo left the show controversially but have since enjoyed a successful career that has seen them win two awards at the South African Traditional Music Awards. The Afro-pop duo from KwaZulu-Natal, who not only share a striking resemblance but also have amazing personalities, are signed under Afrotaiment Records and have released two studio albums, The Gift of Love (2020) and Words of Hope (2022).
