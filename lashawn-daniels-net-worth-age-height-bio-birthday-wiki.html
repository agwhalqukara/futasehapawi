Lashawn Daniels Biography

Lashawn Daniels is one of the most popular and richest Songwriter who was born on December 28, 1977 in Newark, New Jersey, United States. Songwriter behind platinum classics performed by artists like Whitney Houston and Michael Jackson, he’s been called the “Secret Weapon” behind many of pop’s biggest hit songs like Destiny Child’s “Say My Name” and The Spice Girls’ “Forever.”

Daniels died on September 3, 2019, at the age of 41, following a car crash in South Carolina.
