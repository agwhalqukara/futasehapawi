Know About Taryn Manning; Age, Husband, Movies, Height, Net Worth

Published Date: 27 Jul, 2019 Updated date: 27 Jul, 2019 By Celebs Podium

The fans of Taryn Manning were worried as she was not present at the red carpet of the of Orange Is the New Black series on 26 July 2019. She later revealed in an Instagram post stating that she became “paralyzed” with fear after being “terrorized by cyber criminals.”

Taryn Manning is an American actress, who earned her prominence in the role of Rebecca Peabody in the 1999 comedy-drama series, Get Real. Currently, she appears in a lead role as Tiffany “Pennsatucky” Doggett in a Netflix’s series, Orange Is the New Black since 2013.

Taryn Manning Bio; Family, Education, Height

Taryn Gail Manning was born on 6 November 1978 to parents Bill Manning and Sharyn Louise (White). Her father Bill was a musician who got divorced from her mother in 1979. She was raised by her single mother along with her brother Kellin Manning in Tucson, Arizona.

Standing at a height of 5 feet 2 inches (1.57 meters), Manning attended Los Alamitos High School. Later, she got enrolled at Orange County School of the Arts in Santa Ana, California.

Her Career; Movies, TV Shows

Taryn began her career from the 1999 movie Speedway Junky. She was cast in the lead role alongside Britney Spears in the 2002 movie Crossroads. Some of her movies roles include Dandelion (2004), Zombie Apocalypse (2011), Low Down (2014), Happy Yummy Chicken (2016), and The Murder of Nicole Brown Simpson (2019).

Taryn Manning appears as Tiffany “Pennsatucky” Doggett in a Netflix’s series, Orange Is the New Black (Picture: Bustle)

Moreover, Manning played the lead role as Ivy Chitty in the 2007 action drama series Drive. She also appeared as Mary Ann McGarrett in the TV series, Hawaii Five-0 from 2010 to 2019. Her other works in TV shows include Sons of Anarchy (2008), Burn Notice (2012), Hollywood Game Night (2014), and Hollywood Medium with Tyler Henry (2018).

Net Worth, House

Taryn, who has been active since the 1990s has garnered the astounding net worth from her acting career. She currently enjoys a lavish lifestyle with the net worth of an estimated $3 million.
