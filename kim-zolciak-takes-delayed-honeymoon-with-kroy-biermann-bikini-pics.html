Kim Zolciak Takes Delayed Honeymoon With Kroy Biermann: Bikini Pics

Sun, sand, and fun does wonders for Kim Zolciak! The Bravo reality star took a delayed honeymoon with football player husband Kroy Biermann in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico this week — three years after their TV wedding. "Being on vacation is hard work," the mother of six wrote on Instagram over the July 4th weekend.

"We are finally on our 'honeymoon' almost four years later, four kids later and five years of utter bliss!" Zolciak, 37, wrote. "A vacation isn't really a vacation when you are a mommy/daddy. I have spent more time on FaceTime with my kids than the beach. I miss them so very bad! Hopefully this piña colada will help me to relax."

During their stay, Zolciak and Atlanta Falcons player Biermann — who married in a Don’t Be Tardy . . . special episode in 2011 — kept in touch with their kids, Brielle, Ariana, KJ, Kash, and twins Kaia and Kane — through video chat. "Oh how I miss them," she said of her kids, using the hashtag #blessed.
