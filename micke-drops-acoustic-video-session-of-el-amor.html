Micke Drops Acoustic Video Session Of &quot;El Amor&quot;

It’s been a week since Global Records dropped Micke featuring Antonia’s “El Amor“. Now, they’ve dropped an acoustic video session with Micke, who sings this song, and we are loving every minute of it.

We are hoping that an acoustic version of the song would be released; perhaps with some remixes as well. We can hope. For now, we’ll just play this video on repeat. The song is available to download and stream right now.

Watch Micke’s Acoustic Session Video Of “El Amor” Here:

This acoustic session has a lot of Inna’s Rock The Roof vibes going on, as Micke can be seen on top of a rooftop. He not only shows off his amazing voice, but he’s playing the guitar too; now that’s what we’re talking about.

Stripped back from the original, his voice is completely exposed. This is most definitely not a bad thing because it sounds completely on point. Life happens in mysterious ways, he may not have done well on La Banda or The X Factor USA but now Global Records has him and his voice is outstanding.

Clear, crisp, with so much soul. Micke Moreno is certainly a rising star and a great asset to the Global Records label. Don’t forget to check out our article on the original and the accompanying music video, featuring Antonia.
