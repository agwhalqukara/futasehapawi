Official trailer for 'Sons of Anarchy' spin-off 'Mayans M.C.'

FX treated Sons of Anarchy fans with some goodies premiering the official trailer for its spin-off, Mayans M.C. This is just ahead of their Hall H appearance on Sunday at Comic-Con.

Pardo will portray EZ Reyes who is a former good guy now ex-convict aiming to join the group. Based on the trailer alone, EZ will have to also overcome some messy family history.

Mayans M.C. will premiere September 4 on FX with 10-episode first season.
