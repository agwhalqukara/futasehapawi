NeNe Leakes Explains Why She Likely Wont Come Back To RHOA

During her appearence on The Talk on the 19th of February, the Real Housewives of Atlanta star, NeNe Leakes, shared why she has reservations about signing on for another season for the aforementioned reality television show.

BET reported that NeNe, who first started on the series back in 2008, spoke with the hosts of the talk show and explained that she felt as though Real Housewives of Atlanta was her baby because she started on it over ten years ago and played a crucial role in its popularity.

With that said, NeNe argued that she didn’t want to star on a reality television series where all the cast members are continuously attacking each other with malicious intent. Sharon Osbourne, while stoking the flames of the RHOA feuds, told NeNe that she thought a “nasty girl” was jealous of her.

Without NeNe, Sharon argued, there simply wasn’t a show. According to Leakes, she has to watch the other episodes first and also has to make sure that “nasty girl” won’t be saying mean things that she shouldn’t be saying.

According to BET, “nasty girl” is referring to NeNe’s co-star, Kenya Moore. The outlet explained that much of Season 12 has been very dramatic for NeNe, who has fought with other women while also trying to repair her damaged friendship with Cynthia Bailey, who was once her best friend.

Yesterday, Ricki Mathers reported on comments made by NeNe Leakes, including when she accused Kenya Moore of having a “stank attitude.” NeNe stated that Kenya’s attitude and disposition towards others would be the end of the series if she kept it up.

Leakes argued that Kenya is always telling her that no one likes her, but in actuality, no one likes her. Regarding what she intends to do in the future, NeNe has a variety of entrepreneurial endeavors she has been working on over the years.
